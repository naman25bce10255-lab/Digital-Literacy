Do's

1) Audit your privacy settings regulary to ensure you know exactly who can see your posts.

2) Think before you click 'Post' Ask yourself:"Would i be confortable with a future emplyer seeing this?"

3) Give credit where it's due. Always cite original creators when sharing art, photos, or intellectual property.

4) Use your platform for good. Share your achievements, volunteer work, and professional milestones. 

5) Verify information before sharing. Check the facts to avoid spreading "fake news" or misinformation.

Don'ts

1) Don't post illegal or policy-violating content, such as underage drinking or illicit substances. 

2) Don't engage with "trolls" or online bullies. Getting into heated, public arguments rarely ends well. 

3) Don't share sensitive personal info, like your home address, current GPS location, or student ID number.

4) Don't vent about your professors or current jobs. These posts are often screenshotted and shared. 

5) Don't post "complaining" or overly negative content that might make you seem difficult to work with.
